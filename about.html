Who We Are

Your trusted partner for home appliance repairs, bringing premium urban services straight to your doorstep in Gurugram and surrounding areas.

Founded with a vision to organize the unorganized appliance repair sector, Electra Appliances ensures that you don't have to deal with untrained mechanics or inflated pricing. We provide a seamless, tech-enabled booking experience coupled with world-class, on-ground service delivery.

Whether it's a leaking AC in the middle of summer or a broken washing machine on a busy weekend, our team of background-verified, certified technicians is equipped to handle it all efficiently and professionally.

Our Mission

To provide fast, transparent, and high-quality appliance repair services that prioritize customer convenience and extend the lifespan of household appliances.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted and reliable tech-driven platform for home appliance maintenance, setting the gold standard for service quality.
